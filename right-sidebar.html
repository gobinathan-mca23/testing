And Yet Another Subheading

Most organizations make investments in cybersecurity technology to protect their organizations. However, many overlook security alerts generated by these systems and fail to respond in a timely manner to avert incidents. Due to the sheer volume of alerts security and IT teams regularly receive, due lack of capacity and bandwidth, due to the inability to hire and retain security experts needed for respond 24x7;organizations fail to detect and respond to attacks proactively. SOC-as-a-Service / Managed Detection and Response (MDR) services deliver 24/7 threat monitoring, detection and response services to customers, leveraging a combination of next generation technologies, advanced analytics, threat intelligence, and human expertise in incident investigation and response. Scybers SOC-as-a-Service gives you a fully managed security operations 24 hours a day, by combining industry-leading security platforms with our experienced security analysts to hunt, identify and eradicate adversaries.

As you rely more on digital infrastructure to run your business, an effective cybersecurity strategy is key to protecting your business operations, data, reputation,intellectual property, staff and customers. Prudent organizations know that getting the cybersecurity strategy right is a balancing act. Instead of putting all their resources and efforts to prevent breaches, they take on a balanced approach to focus on resilience and embrace an “assume breach”, “zero trust” approach to cybersecurity. Our Cybersecurity Assessments are designed for you to understand the maturity of your cybersecurity practices against leading practices, standards and models. A comprehensive assessment will provide you with a view to maturity of your current practices, cyber risks facing your organization and a base point to start a roadmap to move to a more acceptable level of cyber risks for your organization.
